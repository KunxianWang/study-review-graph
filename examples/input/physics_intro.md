# Intro Mechanics Notes

Newton's second law states that force equals mass times acceleration.

F = m * a

Symbols:
- F: net force
- m: mass
- a: acceleration

Use this law when the mass is treated as constant and the motion can be modeled with a net force.

Kinetic energy is another core relationship:

E_k = 1/2 * m * v^2

Symbols:
- E_k: kinetic energy
- v: velocity

When velocity increases, kinetic energy grows quadratically.
